I am pursuing a Ph.D. at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) within the Machine Learning for Smart Mobility (MLSM) research group under the supervision of Prof. Francisco Camara Pereira and Assoc. Prof. Filipe Rodrigues. The Ph.D. topic is at the intersection between Machine Learning and Electrical Vehicle charging demand. I aim to create a sustainable future through applied research on problems worth solving!
In the spring of 2022 I was on a longer research stay at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS) under Prof. Jessika Trancik.

If you are interested in doing a Master's or Bachelor's thesis in Machine Learning, Electric Vehicles, and Transportation Systems, feel free to contact me.

If you are interested in any of my research, please get in touch with me.

Research interest

My broad research interests is Bayesian modelling and Uncertainty estimation. My main research area is Machine Learning for demand modelling under uncertainty, which I appliy to create intelligent and sustainable transportation systems
